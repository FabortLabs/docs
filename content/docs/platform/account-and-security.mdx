User settings apply to your account across organizations. They are separate from organization settings and team permissions.

## Profile

The Profile page lets you:

- Change your display name.
- Upload, replace, or remove your avatar.
- Review your username, email address, user ID, account status, and creation date.

Username, email address, and user ID are read-only in the current profile interface.

## Password

Open **User Settings** to change the password used for sign-in. Enter the current password, then enter and confirm a new password of at least eight characters.

Changing the password requires all three fields and matching new-password values.

## Active sessions

The Devices page lists active login sessions with their device or browser description, IP address when available, last-seen time, expiry time, and whether the session is the current device.

You can:

- Refresh the session list.
- Log out one session.
- Log out all sessions.

Logging out the current session redirects to sign-in. Logging out all sessions also ends the current session.

## Account creation

When public account creation is available, the sign-up form accepts a display name, username, email address, and password. Usernames must be 3–20 characters and contain only letters, numbers, and underscores. Passwords must be 8–100 characters.

Deployment configuration may place new registrations on a waitlist instead of creating an account immediately. Follow the status shown by the sign-up flow for the active Fabort deployment.
