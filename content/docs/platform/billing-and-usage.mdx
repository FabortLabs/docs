Billing and usage are organization-scoped. Switch to the intended organization before changing a plan or reviewing consumption.

## Billing account

The Billing page combines:

- Billing email, account currency, and tax metadata.
- Available plans and their limits.
- Recurring and metered price items.
- Current subscriptions and their status.
- Wallet balance and top-up controls when returned by the billing service.

Tax metadata is entered as JSON and must be valid before it can be saved. Currency is normalized to uppercase.

## Plans and subscriptions

Fabort's catalog can contain free, subscription, and usage-based plans. A plan can include a recurring price, metered prices, included quantities, and feature limits.

From Billing you can start a plan, switch from the current plan, schedule cancellation, or resume a scheduled cancellation. The checkout flow reviews the plan, collects billing details, and performs the configured payment step.

<Callout type="info" title="Current payment behavior">
  The current frontend interface labels plan payment as simulated. Do not treat the UI confirmation alone as evidence of an external payment processor charge.
</Callout>

Wallet top-ups are entered in the organization's billing currency. The interface converts the entered major-unit amount to minor units before creating the top-up payment.

## Usage

The Usage page reports metered consumption for the current billing period. Each metric can include:

- Used quantity and unit
- A configured limit and percentage consumed
- Cost and currency
- Billing-period start and end dates

Usage is an observation of current metered consumption. Project Settings controls resource caps, while the organization's plan supplies account entitlements; these are related but separate views.
